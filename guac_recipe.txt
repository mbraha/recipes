Ingredients:
 - 3 haas avocados
 - 3 cloves garlic
 - 1/2 cup cilantro
 - cumin (to taste)
 - half of 1 lime
 - mango-peach salse (amonut to taste)
 - salt

Directions:
 1. Using a mortar and pestle, grind garlic, then cilantro, then avocado to a smooth consistency
 
 2. Incorporate cumin, salt, and salsa.
 
 3. Top with salsa and lime, refrigerate for at least 30 min, serve.
